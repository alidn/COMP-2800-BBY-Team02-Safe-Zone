Takashi Asaoka

We want to raise awareness of the impact that social distancing plays on the COVID-19 virus. In order for us as humans to defeat this virus without a lot of damage done, we must take action before it is too late. This game intends to challenge the user to see how long they can survive without social distancing measures vs with. We hope that everybody gets a chance to educate themselves about the virus with our study page and to use this time to be with their families. Some of us do not have that luxury and are in a constant worry, hoping that they are okay. All we can do is hope that we can get our message clearly across so that others can also be at ease, knowing that everything will be okay.
